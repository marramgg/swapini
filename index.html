How it works

1 · Album — open your book and mark what you own. Tap a slot, or hold and drag to paint a run. Own most of a team? Tap only the ones you're missing, then hit ⇄ Invert all 20 to flip the whole team. The layout mirrors each album spread, so match the pattern, not the numbers.

2 · Dupes — once a card is owned, a − 0 + strip appears on it: mark spare copies right there. The Dupes tab lists them A→Z and has a fast keyboard entry mode.

3 · Swap — copy your link and send it on WhatsApp. Paste a friend's link to instantly see who gives what. To sync phone to laptop, send the link to yourself as a "note to self" on WhatsApp.

Quick entry (keyboard)

Same syntax, but here it marks cards, not spares. Press a, f, r or i anywhere to start typing. Space between stickers, double space to finish. ×N in add mode means N copies — the first fills the album, the rest become spares.

atpor5 4 6x2␣␣  → add POR 5, 4, and 6 with one spare
apor5 bel2 alg8␣␣  → add POR 5, BEL 2, ALG 8
ftpor5 6 8␣␣  → jump to Portugal, outline 5, 6, 8 — green owned, red missing
itpor␣␣  → invert all 20 of Portugal

How it works

Mark duplicates on the Album tab: every owned card shows a − 0 + strip. + adds a spare, − removes one, tapping the number clears back to zero. This tab lists your spares by team code, A→Z — the order you'd check against a friend's stack.

Quick entry is for typing with a keyboard. Start with a mode letter, separate stickers with a space, finish with a double space:

a add  ·  f find  ·  r remove  ·  add t for team mode

apor5x2␣␣  → add POR 5, 2 spares
fpor5␣␣  → find POR 5 — owned? spares?
atpor5x2 6x4 20 15x2␣␣  → team mode: POR 5×2, 6×4, 20×1, 15×2
rpor5 6x2␣␣  → remove one POR 5 and two POR 6
